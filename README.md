## Project Overview

Healthcare organizations process thousands of insurance claims every month, making it difficult to quickly identify spending trends, provider performance, diagnosis costs, and insurance utilization.

This project demonstrates how SQL and Google BigQuery can transform raw healthcare claims data into an executive dashboard that supports informed business decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Healthcare administrators need a centralized dashboard that can quickly answer questions such as:

- Which providers generate the highest claim amounts?
- What percentage of claims are approved?
- Which diagnosis categories generate the highest costs?
- Which insurance plans receive the largest claim amounts?
- Which states generate the highest healthcare spending?

---

## Dataset

Synthetic healthcare dataset

- 75,000 insurance claims
- 5,000 patients
- 10 providers
- 5 insurance plans
- Multiple diagnosis categories

## Key Insights

- 75,000 claims analyzed
- $110.28M total claim amount
- Average claim amount of $1,470
- Average processing time of 24.5 days
- Cardiology generated the highest claim spending
- Silver insurance plans produced the highest total claim amount
